California Monthly Housing Cost/Latinx Below Poverty Level, 2010-2018

By Brian Basaldua

Description:
Heatmap of California showing Median Monthly Housing Costs and Number of Latinx people whose income was below the poverty level.
Census data is very valuable and allows us to make neat visualizations like this one to easily identify correlations between data.
In this case, one might find correlation between monthly housing costs and how it affects the number of Latinx people who are below the poverty level.

All data taken from Census API, specifically from the American Communtiy Survey data for 2010-2018

Specific data sources include:

  • Median Monthly Housing Costs in U.S Dollars
  • Number of Hispanic or Latino people whose income was below the poverty level
